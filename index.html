About the Workshop

Sobre el Taller

This workshop brings together international experts working on the geology of Ecuador and the northern Andes, covering tectonics, magmatism, sedimentary basins, and natural resources.

Este taller reúne a expertos internacionales en la geología del Ecuador y los Andes del norte, cubriendo tectónica, magmatismo, cuencas sedimentarias y recursos naturales.
